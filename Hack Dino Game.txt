How to "hack" the Google Chrome Dino Game ?
Step 1
Open the game by typing chrome://dino in your Chrome Browser
Step 2
Open the Developer Mode by pressing Ctrl + Shift + I simultaneously
Step 3
Go to Console and type - Runner.prototype.gameOver = ()=>{};
Step 4
Close the Developer Mode and Start the Game
Step 5
BOOM! That's it ! The dino goes past all without getting killed by cactus and birds
